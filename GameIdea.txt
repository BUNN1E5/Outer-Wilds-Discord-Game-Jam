Idea :: Themes Used :: Isolation, 14.3 Billion Years
	- On a research vessel near a black hole lets say Gaia BH1 (The closest black hole to earth)
		- Story/Setting:
			- You are a researcher on a vessel orbiting a black hole, everything is fine for X minutes when suddenly
				the entire ship shakes, alarms go off you are now falling into a black hole instead of being in a stable orbit
			- We have X minutes before we reach the even horizon, those X minutes will cover billions of years
			- We are doomed, there is nothing we can do to escape our fate, thrusters are dead...
			-
			- As you get closer to the event horizon the stars start to turn blue
				- Except behind you which turn red...
			- You enter the photon sphere
			- You have hit the event horizon, everything goes quiet. The alarms stop, the lights stop flashing
			- After about 30 seconds, everything starts to stretch toward the black hole then everything goes black

							- Limited TOTAL power (we wont ever run out, but we can only use a couple things at once)
							- Limited Packet size (what we are able to send in a single message)
							- What else...
